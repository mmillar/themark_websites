Client Objective

Assess interest in a proposed government-sponsored network of elite Canadian business people abroad.

Tactics

Recruited 100 top Canadian business leaders working overseas to participate in a 3-day online discussion

Created a microsite featuring articles, videos, and debates written by and featuring members of the top 100 to generate discussion/comments from the community

Summarized insights from the online discussion into a report
